PiedPiper Cards

This year has been a tough and weird year, to say the least. Nobody can go out and everybody has been stuck inside their houses and ordering things online. Due to this Online shopping has increased many fold and the businesses are booming. However as people are ordering from many websites, they have to create many accounts for each website and have to connect all these to their credit cards resulting in chaos and confusion. We need a common uniform payment system across all e-commerce websites.

Intoducing PiedPiper Cards, a project that aims to bring all e-commerce platforms to a unified platform. The customers can order all their favourite products from all over various e-commerce platforms from a single website with a single card, without any hassle.
